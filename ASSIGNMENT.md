## Assignment Agreement

All contributions to this repository are subject to the SimpleMotion Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement. By contributing to this repository, you agree to assign all intellectual property rights in your contributions to SimpleMotion.

## Terms

1. **Assignment**: You hereby assign to SimpleMotion all rights, title, and interest in and to any contributions you make to this repository.

2. **Scope**: This includes all copyrights, patent rights, trade secrets, and other intellectual property rights.

3. **Moral Rights**: To the extent permitted by law, you waive any moral rights in your contributions.

4. **Representations**: You represent that:
